Call for Papers

The 6th Computer Science Graduate Research Symposium (CSGRS) will be held on April 29th, in Houston, Texas, USA The conference provides a platform for researchers, scientists and engineers in various domains, in fields such as academics, industry, and government, to present their current findings in the emerging areas of computer science.

Templates

Please use IEEE Conference Proceedings format for submission of your works.

For further details, please take a look at the IEEE Manuscript Templates.

Your submission should be at most 6 pages including references. All submissions are double-blind.

Paper submission link: HotCRP

Research Posters

We solicit posters that articulate research challenges, work in progress, and application showcases of computer science.

Please use one of the following templates to submit your work:

  1. Template 1
  2. Template 2
  3. Template 3
